Resort of the King


WELCOME

Welcome to King Lê Resort where remarkable landscapes meet tribal heritages. Located among three ethnic groups deep in a tropical forest, King Lê Resort represents the inseparable connection between nature and people.

heritage

Lê Thái Tổ Tên húy là Lê Lợi, sinh năm 1385, mất năm 1433, thủ lĩnh cuộc khởi nghĩa Lam Sơn, đánh đuổi quân Minh, giành lại độc lập, sáng lập nhà Hậu Lê. Ông được đánh giá là một vị vua vĩ đại và là anh hùng giải phóng dân tộc trong lịch sử nước ta. Đương thời ông tự xưng là Bình Định vương.

“Act first, think later” was what our founders had in mind when they decided to acquire this 15-hectare area. Although they had no real vision of what they wanted to do with it, they knew that somehow they wanted to preserve the natural beauty and local heritage from the kind of mass-tourism development that has overrun other beautiful, natural spots in Vietnam.

accommodation

My wish is to stay always like this, living quietly in the corner of nature

Claude Monet

The story began with a stunning waterfall, running through the area bringing life with it. Thirty-six gorgeous villas have been nestled on the mountainside . Inspired by ethnic stilt houses, they blend seamlessly into a backdrop of imposing forest, terrace rice fields, and the vast valley below. Ethnic craftsmen were invited to contribute their knowledge to this beautiful property and showcased their talent in the villa’s earth walls, rattan ceiling, and thatched roof.
