Cuisine

Indulge yourself in the Avana culinary experience. Our restaurants support local, seasonal produce to create one-of-a-kind menus. Guests can choose the charming Green Chili Restaurant with sophisticated dishes or Cloud Pool Bar where they can unwind with a signature cocktail or premium glass of wine.

We Serve Only The Finest Ingredients

Dining at our restaurants is a special treat of Vietnam’s finest seasonal ingredients. Most of the ingredients used in our kitchen are locally grown in and around Mai Chau and sourced from the best of the country’s specialty suppliers to support Vietnamese farmers. We have our chicken farm where we let our free-range laying hens roam freely outdoors, molt naturally, and be organically fed. We collect the freshly-laid organic eggs early in the morning and bring them to Green Chili Restaurant to prepare a good-quality breakfast for Avana’s guests.

Green Chili Restaurant

Located at the foot of the mountain with a view of beautiful cascades running gently from the waterfall nearby, Green Chili Restaurant serves both local and fusion dishes to satisfy the most demanding appetite. We take the best ingredients, prepare them simply and beautifully, then let them do the talking. Relish our hearty cooked breakfast that includes homemade bread and jam, and fresh organic eggs. Return in the evening to be dazzled by delicious dishes featured in our à la carte menu. Inspired by the region's specialties and the most common herbs and spices found in every Vietnamese northern mountain kitchen: our food creates memorable experiences and provides a unique sense of place through its presentation and culinary perfection.

Cloud Pool Bar

Perfectly located right next to the elegant three-level infinity pool, our Cloud Pool Bar has a panoramic view of the valley, terraced rice fields, and the waterfall. We serve delectable lunches and small plates to satisfy afternoon cravings. At sunset, enjoy magical views from the outdoor terrace as you sip a glass of Fingers of the Dragon cocktail - a perfect blend of vodka, apricot brandy, and marinated artichoke blossom to create a complex flavor profile.

Private Dining

Honeymoon, anniversary or just an occasion to surprise your special someone, there are magical moments to be had at Avana. From decadent in-villa dining or a champagne picnic to a romantic dinner by the waterfall, your special dining experience is waiting to unfold.
