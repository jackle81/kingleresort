Start your journey to Avana Retreat the fastest way

The vast and cool nature at Avana Retreat always welcomes you to enjoy a relaxing vacation. But do you know how to get to Avana Retreat? The information below can do some help!

Where Avana Retreat lies

Located in Panh hamlet, Bao La commune, a remote place of Mai Chau, Hoa Binh, Avana Retreat is not difficult to access, as the road leading to the resort is convenient for both private cars and 45-seat passenger cars to pass by.

Distance from Avana Retreat to nearby points

The resort locates in a very convenient spot: not too far from Hanoi, close to Son La, bordering Thanh Hoa to the west and Ninh Binh to the south, so you can move to Avana Retreat easily and combine exploring the famous destinations of the north.

With departures from the central and southern regions, moving to Hanoi, then to Avana Retreat will be the most convenient option.

Noi Bai airport

Do not forget to save the distance from Avana Retreat to nearby points:

- Distance from Pu Luong (Thanh Hoa): 50km (1.5 hours by car)

- Distance from Moc Chau town (Son La): 90km (2 hours by car)

- Distance from Ninh Binh city: 160km (4 hours by car)

- Distance from Hanoi city: 165km (4 hours by car)

- Distance from Noi Bai Airport (Hanoi): 185km (4 hours by car)

- Distance from Cat Bi airport (Hai Phong): 290km (6 hours by car)

- Distance from Ha Long city: 315km (6 hours by car)

How to get to Avana Retreat

Private car service provides a comfortable travel experience while taking your mind off your worries about how to get to Avana Retreat. Depending on your needs and budget, Avana Retreat will offer an appropriate cost. Feel free to send information to the following email to book a private car service: inquiries@avanaretreat.com
