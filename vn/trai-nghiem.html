In many places of Vietnam, agriculture is highly labor-intensive and parts of plowing is still done by water buffalo. The picture of a buffalo on the paddy field evokes a sense of the peaceful country life in Vietnam and reminds many elders of their childhood.

Learn Vietnamese traditional farming with a local farmer. Our gentle buffaloes will be your company to make sure you have a fun time.
