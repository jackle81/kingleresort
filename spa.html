Orchid Spa

The tranquil Orchid Spa resembles a cool rainforest where calming sounds of the waterfall and the earthy scent of trees & flowers follow your every step.

spa brochure

One-of-a-kind journey

With a full menu of body treatments and facials targeted to each guest’s needs, Orchid Spa is a wellness sanctuary that puts the focus on indulgence and relaxation. Eight private treatment rooms perfectly blend into nature and deliver a completely gratifying and relaxing experience.

Begin the journey with a seasonal drink while choosing your preferred fragrant herb to be used in your treatment. Unwind as you lay back and feel your tensions evaporate as your mind and body are pampered and rejuvenated.
